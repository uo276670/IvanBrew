The shaman

Overview

Shamans are magical users who perform old magic. They channel nature through their bodies, thats why their main characteristic is CONSTITUTION.

They can only fight up close or perform old rituals that sometimes tax their bodies in the form of expending hit die

  • LvL1: Ritual Casting

    Allows you to perform Rituals of magic using your body as the focus. You can cast Rituals as many times you like.

    When you take the ritual action, you begin concentrating on your ritual and need to keep your concentration for the whole duration of its performance in order to complete it.

    On your next turns you need to spend your action to continue your ritual until it is completed. When you perform a rite during combat, the amount of actions needed to perform the ritual can be seen on the rite table

    Besides regular concentration rules. Moving, or being forced to move while you perform a rite will end your concentration.

    If you lose your concentration or you cannot use your action to continue the ritual, the rite will fail losing all the progress towards performing the ritual spell

  • LvL4: Ability score upgrade

    You gain an ability score upgrade or a feat. You gain the same benefict at levels 8, 12, 16, 19

  • LvL10: Ritual Body

    While you are casting a ritual, you can force the energy through yourself to finish rituals earlier.

    During a turn where you are performing a rite, you can spend one of your hit die to reduce the number of actions required to finish the rite. For each die you spend, you reduce it by 1 action

    You then take damage for each hit die used and roll separatedly to maintain concentration

  • LvL18: Ritual Mind

    While you are casting a ritual, you have advantage on constitution saves to maintain your concentration

  • LvL20: Ritual Spirit

    When you begin a fight you recover a number of hit die equal to your CON modifier up to your maximum. Your first spell takes 1 action to perform.

Spells

You have the ability of performing rituals to invoke magic. These rituals do not any spell slot. In order to cast a ritual you have to spend twice the casting time while concentrating on the spell standing still

If you move or are forced to move, the ritual ends. Breaking your concentration while performing the ritual also cancels it.

You know a number of rituals equal to your proficiency bonus. CON is your ability to perform those rituals. The rituals you perform can be upgraded using the rite abilities

Every time you perform a healing ritual of any kind, you have to spend a number of your hit die equal to your ritual power or gain one level of exhaustion in case you dont have any hit dice left
