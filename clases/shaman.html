The shaman

Overview

Shamans are magical users who perform old magic. They channel nature through their bodies, thats why their main characteristic is CONSTITUTION.

They can only fight up close or perform old rituals that sometimes tax their bodies in the form of expending hit die

A shaman has to think in advance since they need at best 2 turns to cast any spell. When they perform a new ritual they lost the concentration and effects of the previous rite, so it is important that they manage a spell untill it is dismised to then start a new rite

Stats

  • Hit die D12
  • you are proficient with unarmed, clubs and quarterstafs
  • You cannot wear armor
  • Select 2 from: medicine, nature, survival, animal handling, insight
  • Saves: wisdom, constitution

  • LvL1: Ritual Casting

    Allows you to perform Rituals of magic as many times as you want. Your ability for these rituals is CON.

    Rituals take a long time to make. When you take the ritual action, the next turns you will be stuck finishing the ritual. Each turn after you take the ritual action, you can use your action to continue the ritual. When you continue the ritual this way, you throw one D12. The ritual will be done the moment you add up a number greater that or equal to tree times the ritual level.

    E.G you are a level 5 shaman and take the Perform a Rite action to perform a Thunderwave ritual. Since you are lvl 5, the thunderwave will be level 3. Then you will need a 9 to end the ritual. On your next turn you take the Continue Ritual action, throw a D12 and get a 4. On your next turn you do the same and get a 6. The total sum is 10 so the ritual is completed and Thunderwave is casted

    When you perform a ritual, you gain concentration on it and cannot move while performing the ritual. In case you decide to move, or are forced to move, you lose the concentration

    When you use a ritual that heals a creature in any way, you have to spend a number of your hit die equal to your rite power. In case you dont have enough hit die left, you gain a level of exhaustion.

Spells

You have the ability of performing rituals to invoke magic. These rituals do not any spell slot. In order to cast a ritual you have to spend twice the casting time while concentrating on the spell standing still

If you move or are forced to move, the ritual ends. Breaking your concentration while performing the ritual also cancels it.

You know a number of rituals equal to your proficiency bonus. CON is your ability to perform those rituals. The rituals you perform can be upgraded using the rite abilities

Every time you perform a healing ritual of any kind, you have to spend a number of your hit die equal to your ritual power or gain one level of exhaustion in case you dont have any hit dice left
