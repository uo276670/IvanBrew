The shaman

Overview

Shamans are magical users who perform old magic. They channel nature through their bodies, thats why their main characteristic is CONSTITUTION.

They can only fight up close or perform old rituals that sometimes tax their bodies in the form of expending hit die

Actions required 222233344

  • Lvl1: Healing remedy

    You can help others heal thanks to themselves. You can spend one action so that one creature of your choice you treat can use one of their hit die to recover health. They heal for a throw of their hit die + your medicine modifier

  • LvL1: Ritual Casting

    Allows you to perform Rituals of magic using your body as the focus. You can cast Rituals as many times you like but cannot be upcasted.

    When you take the ritual action, you begin performing your ritual and need to keep doing it for the whole duration in order to complete it.

    On your next turns you need to spend your action to continue your ritual until it is completed. When you perform a rite during combat, the amount of actions needed to perform the ritual can be seen on the rite table

    If you move, are forced to move or you cannot use your action to continue the ritual, the rite will fail losing all the progress towards performing the ritual spell

    You select a number of rites from your spell list acording to the Rites Known column on the table. The spells you choose must be at most of the level shown in Rite Power column. You can spend time during your short or long rest contacting the spiritual forces to change the rites you have prepared

  • LvL4: Ability score upgrade

    You gain an ability score upgrade or a feat. You gain the same benefict at levels 8, 12, 16, 19

  • LvL10: Enhanced rituals

    Each turn you are casting a ritual, you can use your bonus action and spend one of your hit die to upgrade the ritual in some way

    You can increase the rite's damage by 1D12 for each hit die you use

    You can lower one enemy's spell dc save by 1D12 per dice used. You can use this upgrade multiple times for 1 target, or you can select multiple targets

    The moment you spent a hit die this way, you already used it even if the rite doesnt take place

  • LvL14: Spiritual senses

    Your ties to the spiritual world makes you see things others dont. You can see spirtis and you can try to interact with them in order to try and comunicate

    You can detect high spiritual activity from afar

  • LvL18: Ritual body

    While you are casting a ritual, you have resistance to all damage thanks to the protective energies that surround you

  • LvL20: Ritual Spirit

    Once per encounter, when you complete a ritual, the spell is cast twice, targeting the same or different targets.
