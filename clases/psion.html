The Psion

Overview

I like the idea of psions being focused on the mind, specifically the part of concentration while being based on low hp type of class

I like the Idea of having a limited ability of concentrating on 2 Spells at the shame time at lvl 5 but they receive x2 damage and if they lose concentration they lose both spells

I also like the Idea of having special things to do WHEN you are concentrating or another enemy is concentrating

Stats

  • Hit die D6
  • No armor or weapon proficiency
  • Select 2 from: history, investigation, perception, insight, persuation, intimidation, deception
  • Saves: Intelligence, Wisdom

  • LvL1: Psionics

    The way you use your brain and mind to perform magical feats. You know the Mage Hand cantrip, it is invisible when you cast it and it doesnt count towards the amount of cantrips known. Also, when you cast a spell from your psionic spell list, you can forgo verbal and somatic components of the spell

    A psionic spell is any spell learnt directly through this class or subclass. If you learn spell by any other mean like Feat:Magic Initiate, those spells doesnt count as psionic

    In order to maintain you concentration on the spells you have to make an INT save when you take damage with DC10 or the amount of damage you got halved, whichever is bigger.

    Since your mind is the focus of your magic, you cannot cast spells from your list of psionic spells if your mind is being mendled with.

    Conditions that disable your magic: charmed, frightened, incapacitated, stunned, unconscious or under the effects of the spell "calm emotions"

  • LvL1: Mind Fatigue

    Multiple features of this class requires to spend a lot of physical and mental energy to perform. This is shown with the Mind Fatigue.

    When a feature requires you to suffer Mind Fatigue, choose and expend one of your remaining hit dice. Roll it. You lose that die and take damage equal to the result. This damage ignores resistance, immunity, and temporary hit points, and cannot be reduced or avoided in any way.

  • LvL2: Mind Amplification

    While you are concentrating on one or more spells, you get bonuses depending on the number of spells. a spell, you get +1AC per your spells deal an extra 1D6 of its damage type per spell being simultaneaosly concentrated.

    For each spell you are concentrating on, you get +1AC and damaging spells have their damage increased by 1D6.

    The damage increase is part of the spell, its not a separate instance of damage. Then the 1D6 shares damage type with the spell. You can think of this like upcasting a spell so that it deals more damage.

    Concentrate on 1 spell = 1d6 extra damage and +1AC. Concentrate on 3 spell = 3d6 extra damage and +3AC

  • LvL3: Heightened Mind

    By means of forcing your mind, you can concentrate better. When you cast a spell that requires concentration while you are already concentrating on another spell, you can give yourself Mind Fatigue to maintain concentration on both spells at the same time.

    All damage you take is increased by 1D6 while you concentrate on more than one spell. In case you lose concentration, both spells end inmediately and you take 1d6 psychic damage.

  • LvL4: Ability score upgrade

    You gain an ability score upgrade or a feat. You gain the same benefict at levels 8, 12, 16, 19

  • LvL6: Brainstorm

    You can use your bonus action to end your concentration on all spells to deal damage. You can select a single target at 60ft, or you can make a sphere of 5ft centered at yourself. All target creatures make a save

    The damage is a number of D6 equal to the number of spell being concentrated on simultaneously

    Note that cantrips count as level 0 spells and therefore do not contribute towards this feature's damage. A creature that doesnt pass this check takes half damage. Choose either force damage (targets make a Constitution save) or psychic damage (targets make an Intelligence save) when you activate this feature.

  • LvL10: Complex mind

    Choose one skill from the Psion class skill list in which you are proficient. You gain expertise in that skill, doubling your proficiency bonus for any ability check you make with it.

    You are immune to being charmed, and your thoughts cannot be read by any means, including spells such as detect thoughts or telepathy. Additionally, creatures cannot target you with effects that would read, manipulate, or alter your thoughts or emotions unless you choose to allow it.

  • LvL14: Ascended mind

    You can use your Heightened Mind feature to concentrate on 3 spells at the same time. When you concentrate on 3 spells, your movement speed is halved and, you must get Mind Fatigue at the start of each of your turns

    You cannot posibly concentrate on more than 3 psionic spells

    You can forgo any of the three spells at any time as normal with concentration spells. If you start your turn without hit die, you lose concentration

    If you are forced to lose concentration (because enemy attack, dispell magic or if you start turn without hit die) while on ascended mind, you skip your next turn and take 1D6 damage

  • LvL18: Psionic grasp

    When you cast a spell, you can use your bonus action to increse you spell dc or your spell attack roll. You can get yourself Mind Fatigue to increase the spell or dc by the amount rolled.

  • LvL20: Focused mind

    When you lose your concentration you can spend 1 hit die, take damage equal to the result of the dice, and not lose concentration. Each time you use this feature, the amount of hit die used increases by 1. Resets after long rest
