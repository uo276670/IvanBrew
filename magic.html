What I dont like

All magic feels the same regardless of where it comes from. I would love for each class to have a different relationship with how it all works.

What I like

I feel that the general way magic works, with the components and concentrations and rituals spell slots, etc... it is all very understandable and I like how you are able to go from one class to another and more or less understand how it works

If we take that conceps and make different classes be more focused on some part in order to make them feel cooler

Examples

  • Shamans that are more focused on rituals. And creating buffs. They spend multiple turns casting a single very powefull spell
  • Wizards can create, mix and learn most things and are more or less a master of all trades regarding magic.
  • Warloks are spell shotguns but the pact should be a bigger part of the them. I would make them have spell slots equal to proficiency and more buffs from their patrons
  • Bards are based on multiple target effects with all that music and poetry and all that.
  • Paladins have lots of concentration spells

These are my main ideas. But in general I think that each class should have very specific gameplay mechanics and focus and goals during a fight that other classes dont really care about that much
